Career Paths

Where Will HRM Take You?

Explore the many directions your HR career can take — each path offers unique challenges and rewards.

In-Demand

Talent Acquisition Specialist

Source, attract, and hire top talent. Build employer branding strategies and manage end-to-end recruitment pipelines.

Growing

Learning & Development Manager

Design training programs, upskill workforces, and create continuous learning cultures within organizations.

Strategic

Compensation & Benefits Analyst

Develop competitive pay structures, manage benefits programs, and ensure equitable compensation practices.

Essential

Employee Relations Manager

Mediate workplace conflicts, ensure labor law compliance, and foster positive employee-employer relationships.

Tech-Forward

HRIS Analyst

Manage HR technology systems, optimize people data analytics, and drive digital transformation in HR.

Leadership

Organizational Development Consultant

Drive change management, design organizational structures, and align culture with business strategy.
